Environmental Impacts

Understand the impacts of urban drool. What types of pollutants does it contain, how might it impact wildlife, habitat, and people in the community.

What pollutants does Urban Drool spread?

Fertilizer, Bacteria and Contaminants

Urban drool can carry any pollutants it encounters downstream.

This can include fertilizer, bacteria, and other contaminants that may be present on surface.

By reducing urban drool, you can help reduce the impact of these pollutants.

What animals does Urban Drool affect

Turtles, Woodpeckers, and Roadrunners

The pollutants carried by urban drool can have negative impacts on the wildlife that live along the creeks, canyons, and natural areas in Aliso Creek Watershed.

Species that are present in this watershed include a variety of mammals, birds, reptiles, and amphibians.

What plants does Urban Drool affect?

Blackberries, Deergrass, and Oak Trees

Many plant species thrive in the riparian areas in the Aliso Creek watershed.

Clean water is an important part of a health ecosystem.

What places and habitats does Urban Drool affect?

Aliso Creek, Aliso Beach, and the Pacific Ocean

Aliso Creek has a variety of habitats, from upland communities, to free-flowing Aliso Creek, to the scenic beaches of Pacific Ocean<./p>

By reducing urban drool these natural resources can better support the people and habitat who live nearby.

How does Urban Drool impact people

Health, vibrant communities

Clean water supports all communities in the places they live, work, and play.

By reducing urban drool riparian areas and beaches provide benefits to neighboring communities and visitors.
