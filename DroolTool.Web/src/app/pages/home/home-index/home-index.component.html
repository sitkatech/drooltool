Only rain down the drain

Water that discharges off your property due to overwatering landscape or car washing, picks up fertilizer, bacteria, and other contaminants on its way to creeks and beaches.


This is Urban Drool. And it starts in a gutter near you...


This “Urban Drool” contributes to more than 1 million gallons of polluted discharge to Aliso Creek each day. But small changes in how you use outdoor water can help eliminate urban drool and save you money.


Take action in your community!

Moulton Niguel Water District and the Municipal Water District of Orange County offer a variety of rebate programs you can participate in to reduce the amount of urban drool your household may create.
